Red Velvet (Hangul: 레드벨벳) is a South Korean girl group formed by SM Entertainment.
The group debuted on August 1, 2014, with the digital single "Happiness" and four group members: Irene, Seulgi, Wendy and Joy.
4In March 2015, Yeri was added into the group.

Since their debut, Red Velvet has released two studio albums, one reissue album, and seven extended plays in Korean,
with eight of them topping South Korea's Gaon Album Chart. Their singles "Ice Cream Cake", "Dumb Dumb", "Russian Roulette", "Rookie",
"Peek-a-Boo", "Bad Boy" have all charted in the top five of the Gaon Digital Chart, while their singles "Red Flavor" and "Power Up"
topped the chart upon their release.
